Expression

OrBit uses your movements to take your performance to the next level. It senses motion patterns you teach it and maps it to any digital effect in the universe. Control anything from simple reverb to a buzzing light show with a single device.

Versatility

The instrument you play should not restrict how far you can take your music. Our stage-proof attachment mechanisms mean you can attach OrBit wherever suits you - be that on your instrument or on your body.

Software Integration
