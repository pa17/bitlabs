OrBit turns your motion into music

BitLabs presents OrBit, a motion-sensing device that augments your performance with digital effects. It bridges the gap between acoustic and digital music by giving you access to modern audio effects that you control with your movements. Reverb, delay, pitch harmonisers, vocoders, light shows, all at the flick of your wrist.

One, Two, Three, Go!


1. Attach OrBit to your body or instrument.


2. Connect to your digital audio workstation via Bluetooth.


3. Select the effects to control


Showtime! This is how easy it to augment your performance.

Expression

OrBit captures your movements using motion sensors to take your performance to the next level. You teach it motion patterns, which it can map it to any digital effect. Control anything from simple reverb to a buzzing light show with a single device.

Versatility

The instrument you play should not restrict how far you can take your music. Our stage-proof attachment mechanisms allow you to attach OrBit wherever suits you - whether that's on your instrument or on your body. OrBit comes with a suction cup, clip or wristband attachment.

Software Integration

There are as many digital music effects as there are stars in the night-sky. OrBit gives you access to every single one of them, at no added cost, in a single device. And it integrates with any digital audio workstation. from entry-level to professional.
